Security Level Note


The security level represents the probability of being cracked after 10^12 guesses.

0 : smaller than 10%

1 : range from 10% to 20%

2 : range from 20% to 30%

3 : range from 30% to 40%

4 : range from 40% to 50%

5 : larger than 50%
